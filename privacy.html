Privacy Policy for Quantum AI: Daily Planner

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

1. Overview

Quantum Assistant is a voice-first productivity OS. This policy describes how we handle information to provide our services.

2. Accessibility Service API Disclosure

Our app uses the Accessibility Service API to provide the "Automated Outreach" and "Reply-on-Behalf" features. This allows the app to assist in sending messages or performing task-related actions on your behalf when you are occupied.

  • We do NOT use this API to collect personal data.
  • We do NOT monitor your private screen content.

3. Data Usage

Information processed by the Action Chat (NLP) is used solely to execute user-requested commands such as calendar syncing, task creation, and auto-replies.
